# Eudaimonia Strategy

> Product strategy for the AI-Native Operating System

---

## Executive Summary

Eudaimonia is positioned to pioneer **AI agent infrastructure** - the operating system layer for autonomous AI that works for individuals, not corporations. While competitors build chatbots, we're building AI that has memory, earns money, and acts autonomously.

**Strategic thesis**: The current AI paradigm (stateless assistants serving corporate interests) will be replaced by persistent AI agents that serve individual owners. Whoever builds the best agent operating system captures the agentic AI market.

### Pricing Strategy

#### Pricing Philosophy

1. **Open source core** - Build trust, attract developers
2. **Hosted is convenience premium** - Not value-gated
3. **API Bridge is outcome-based** - Pay for automation value
4. **Enterprise is relationship** - High-touch, custom pricing

#### Competitive Pricing Analysis

| Product | Free | Pro Equivalent | Enterprise |
|---------|------|----------------|------------|
| ChatGPT | Yes (limited) | $20/month | $30/seat |
| Claude | Yes (limited) | $20/month | Custom |
| Auto-GPT | Yes (OSS) | N/A | N/A |
| **Eudaimonia** | Yes (full) | $30/month | $500+/month |

**Rationale**: Premium justified by persistent memory, specialists, and actual automation capability vs. chat-only competitors.
