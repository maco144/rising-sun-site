# GameGames Strategy

> Product strategy for the Decentralized Skill Wagering Platform

---

## Executive Summary

GameGames is positioned to capture the emerging **skill-based wagering** market by being the first truly trustless platform. While competitors require trusting centralized operators, our Solana smart contracts + AI verification create a system where even we cannot manipulate outcomes or freeze funds.

**Strategic thesis**: The $200B+ online gambling market has never had a trustless, decentralized option. As crypto adoption grows and traditional platforms face increasing regulatory pressure, trustless wagering becomes the only sustainable model.

## IV. Regulatory Strategy

### Regulatory Landscape

| Region | Status | Approach |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **US** | Complex, state-by-state | Skill gaming exemptions, geo-blocking risky states |
| **EU** | Variable by country | Malta/Gibraltar licensing (long-term) |
| **UK** | Strict gambling laws | Geo-blocked initially |
| **Asia** | Generally restrictive | VPN users accepted (no KYC) |
| **LATAM** | Permissive | Primary growth market |

### Compliance Strategy

#### Phase 1: Launch (Permissionless)
- No KYC (wallet = identity)
- Geo-block highest-risk jurisdictions
- Clear terms of service
- "Skill gaming" positioning (not gambling)

#### Phase 2: Scaling (Selective Compliance)
- Legal review by jurisdiction
- Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) license consideration
- Age verification for certain markets

#### Phase 3: Mainstream (Full Compliance)
- Multi-jurisdiction licensing
- KYC option for regulated markets
- Traditional payment rails alongside crypto

### Legal Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Unlicensed gambling** | Skill gaming exemption arguments, legal opinion |
| **Money laundering** | Transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reports |
| **Minor access** | Wallet age impractical, community enforcement |
| **Contract disputes** | Smart contract is the contract, immutable |
